Lu Rou Fan

Description

Taiwanese Braised Pork Rice (Lu Rou Fan), sometimes known as Minced Pork Rice, is one of the most popular & iconic Taiwanese street food! It is made using a variety of spices and is often accompanied with egg and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb. boneless pork belly
  • 2 Tbsp. minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp. brown sugar (or rock sugar)
  • 1/2 cup light soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp. rice wine
  • 3 Tbsp. dried shallot
  • 1 tsp. five spice powder
  • 1/2 tsp. white pepper
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 Tbsp. dark soy sauce
  • 4 cups filtered water (or more to cover the meat)
  • eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the pork belly into large pieces, then brown for a few minutes before putting into your slow cooker.
  2. Add all the seasonings and water (just enough to cover the pork ~ approximately 3 3/4 cups of water), then pressure cook on high for 30 minutes.
  3. Add optional medium boiled eggs or other ingredients like bamboo shoots (if so desired).
  4. Serve hot over rice. Spoon a bit of the braising liquid over the pork, eggs, and rice for extra flavor.
